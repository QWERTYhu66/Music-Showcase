About Us

Music Showcase一開始在2017年由幾個家庭協力為自家學習音樂的孩子提供一個沒壓力、可培養信心的表演舞台。

每位用心的家長皆有不同的期盼，但藉由陪伴成長和音樂活動，我們可以一起留下美好的回憶。

目前所有行政事項都由家長義務擔任，使每年的活動越見茁壯和豐富。除了音樂表演，我們也發起了募款活動，並將慢慢讓孩子們接手整場活動的規劃與執行。

最後，

我們這一群學習音樂，一起隨著時間成長的朋友和家庭。希望除了驗證我們的成長，更加重要的是讓大家在歲末年終享有一個輕鬆愉快的時光。

The Music Showcase began in 2017 with families who wanted to provide a platform without pressure for our kids who studies music and build their confidence.

Although each parent might have different expectations and goals for their children, our participation in music activities shall render vivid and lovely childhood memories that serve as a solid foundation and help them conquer all future challenges.

Currently, all administration affairs are handled by parents pro bono. With their great aids, the showcase has been enhanced every year. Apart from the growth in music skills, we also initiated charity donations, setting a good example for our children. We plan to gradually hands-off and let our children take care of the showcase entirely.

Lastly,

As a group of friends and families who loves music, we gather together every year-end to perform music, witness the growth of our children, and most importantly, enjoy our friendship.

History

2017 第一届 由Yoko和 Kevin召集，感謝每位家長用心的參與，使我們的第一屆音樂會能順利舉行。

2018 第二届 家長們培養出合作默契，在群組中互相討論安排，使音樂會能更加完善。

2019 第三届 因應發展，書面制定基本表演原則並發起愛心樂捐給 「親愛愛樂」。

2020 第四届 由Sherianne媽咪擔任統籌，建置工作清單以利分派各項工作事宜 。 此屆開始有小朋友認領工作並開始學習行政事務。今年請小朋友自三個愛心樂捐對象做選擇：「親愛愛樂」、「家扶基金會」和 「天使心」。 選擇的用意係希望小朋友藉由閱讀慈善機構的背景，細思並體會到他們在社會中是幸福的一群。最後以我們的方式幫助資源稀缺的對象。 小朋友樂捐選擇感想影片： https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Tjb6rn06nI

2021 第五屆 由 Karo 媽咪擔任統籌。 網站也從這屆開始發佈，來記錄一次又一次的成長。 和往年的一點不同是今年的場地選擇在東吳大學内舉辦。場地的規格和小孩們的表演也都一起同步升級。再來 Aaron 和 Adrian 今年也擔任了主持人，他們一點都不畏懼上場反而，台風穩健，表現的可圈可點。

2017 - 1st Showcase - Initiated by Yoko and Kevin. All parents were so excited and devoted to our first showcase.

2018 - 2nd Showcase - Our team has grown bigger and stronger. We have developed a rapport, and through the group discussion, enhanced our showcase.

2019 - 3rd Showcase - Principles and rules of performance are established. We have initiated charity donations to “Chin-ai Music.”

2020 - 4th Showcase - Sherianne was in charge of coordinating and developing a working document to record and allocate all admin work.

2021 - 5th Showcase - Coordinated by Karo. The website was also launched starting this showcase to record the growth again and again. One difference from previous years is that this year's venue is chosen to be held in Soochow University. The specifications of the venue and the performances of the children have also been upgraded simultaneously. Aaron and Adrian also served as hosts this year. They were not afraid of going on stage at all. Instead, they were steady and their performances were remarkable.

Starting from 2020, kids began to take over some admin affairs. Also, they were asked to select from 3 recipients (Chi-ai Music, Taiwan Fund for Children and Family, and Angel Heart Family Social Welfare Foundation) and decide who they wanted to donate the most and why: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Tjb6rn06nI. We wished that our kids could benefit from the philanthropy actions and appreciate how lucky they are.
